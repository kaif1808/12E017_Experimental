Task 1 — Piece Rate

In this task you will have 5 minutes to add as many sets of five two-digit numbers as you can. You will see one set at a time. Type your answer and press Enter (or click Submit) to move to the next problem.

Payment

If Task 1 is the task selected for payment, you will earn $0.50 for each correct answer. Incorrect answers are not penalized.

Example

47 23 81 55 12

The correct answer is 218.

The task begins immediately when you click Next. The timer will start and you will not be able to pause it.

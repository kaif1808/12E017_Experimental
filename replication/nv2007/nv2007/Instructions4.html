Task 4 — Apply a Payment Scheme to Your Task 1 Score

There is no new performance task. Instead, you will choose a payment scheme to apply to your Task 1 score.

Option A — Piece Rate

You earn $0.50 for each problem you answered correctly in Task 1.

Option B — Tournament

You earn $2.00 per correct answer from Task 1 — but only if your Task 1 score is higher than the Task 1 scores of the other three participants in your group. If you do not have the highest Task 1 score, you earn $0. Ties are broken randomly.

On the next page, please make your choice.

Task 2 — Tournament

Task 2 is identical to Task 1: you will have 5 minutes to add sets of five two-digit numbers. The task begins immediately when you click Next.

Payment

If Task 2 is selected for payment, you will be paid under a tournament scheme. The participant in your group of four who answers the most problems correctly wins $2.00 per correct answer. All other participants receive $0 for this task.

In the event of a tie, the winner is determined randomly.

You will not be told anyone else's score during the task.

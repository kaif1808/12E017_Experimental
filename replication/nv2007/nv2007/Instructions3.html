Task 3 — Choose Your Payment Scheme

Task 3 is identical to the previous tasks: 5 minutes of addition problems. Before you begin, you must choose how you will be paid if Task 3 is selected for payment.

Option A — Piece Rate

You earn $0.50 per correct answer, regardless of others' performance.

Option B — Tournament

You earn $2.00 per correct answer — but only if your Task 3 score is higher than the Task 2 scores of the other three participants in your group. If you do not have the highest score, you earn $0. Ties are broken randomly.

Note: your Task 3 performance is compared against your groupmates' Task 2 scores, not their Task 3 scores.

On the next page, please make your choice. The task will begin on the page after that.
